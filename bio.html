Biography

My name is Jorge Enrique Nunez, and I am from El Salvador. Life has taught me to learn about my past, also never forget my convictions and dreams, and always look forward to finding meaning in life. I have a passion for the arts, and my dream is to become a graphic designer.
Art is one of my passions in life, and I consider myself a painter. My desire to paint came from going to museums, and then I learned how to paint by watching tutorials on YouTube. This motivates me to know that I am on the right path, doing what I am supposed to do.
There's always time to dream bigger, which is the motto I choose to follow. Whenever I think about art, its obstacles and challenges, it keeps me going. This helps me to keep fighting for what I believe in and am capable of.
