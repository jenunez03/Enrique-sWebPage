How It All Began

I've been fascinated with paint after I had my first college trip at the Metropolitan Museum in NY, USA. I felt enlighted by seeing how art has evolved during the years, and I wanted to be part of it. So I decided to learn as much as I could on tutorials, also mistakes were a great teacher when I started. Now, I strive to work on anything that helps me to grow as a person, and I have realized that working with art is a great way to heal and to meditate on the things sometime we overlook and we ignore.

Description

I try to work with topics that are around my life, thinks I am feeling or expericing. I want to show to people a bit what my inner world is about, sometimes is hard to express with words, but with painting you can tell right away what's going on.

Process

My paintings are made with oil paint making it more vivid, and a bit realistic. However, I've work other mediums like acrylis, watercolor, pastel crayons etc, I always come back to work with oil paint, I feel like it is my thing and I feel more experience with it.
Autumn leaves at Cheshire, CT
October, 2021

Autumn goes away in a blink of an eye. This painting shows one of my favorite places where I have experienced happiness and good people have surrounded me.

I teleport to that place When I see this artwork. Deep recollection, meditation, and peacefulness are what bring up this painting.
